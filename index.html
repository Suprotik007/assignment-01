Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovations

Innovations are new ideas, products, or methods that improve or introduce something new. They can be found in many areas of life, including business, technology, and art.

Sustainability

Sustainability is a social goal that involves using resources responsibly and wisely so that they can be maintained over time.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is composed of public and private physical structures such as roads, railways, bridges, airports, public transit systems, tunnels, water supply, sewers, electrical grids, and telecommunications.

Empowerment

Empowerment means people having power and control over their own lives.
